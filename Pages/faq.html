Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

How does the AI detect skin cancer?

The AI uses advanced algorithms to analyze skin images and identify potential signs of skin cancer. It's based on extensive data and dermatological expertise.

Is my data kept private?

Absolutely. We prioritize user privacy and adhere to strict data protection regulations to ensure your information is secure.

How accurate is the AI diagnosis?

While highly accurate, our AI provides a preliminary assessment and should not replace professional medical advice. We always recommend consulting with a healthcare professional.

Can I use the app for regular skin check-ups?

Yes, the AI Health Detector is an excellent tool for regular monitoring, but it should complement, not replace, regular medical check-ups.
